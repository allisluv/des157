Alli's DES 157 Project Brainstorm

I learned something

Everyone knows that you haven't completely learned something until you can teach it. This would be a platform for young kids (elementary school age) to each other about anything that interests them. They could share things they learn in school, something about the instrument they play, or something they learned with or from their parents. It would create an opportunity for kids to solidify what they know and learn from other kids.

The village

This platform would allow parents to create the village it takes to raise their kids. It would connect 3 parties: parents, local extra curricular programs, and donars who want to help kids get involved with extracurriculars. This would ensure that money is not the reason that a child would be excluded from extracurriculars. It would also help parents connects and organize carpools.

Trash tracker

This would be an app that helps people keep track of what they are buying and how it's packaged. It would take data, analyze it, and display it in a way that shows the impact the user has on the environment from packaging alone. Idealy this app would be able to provide advice on how to lower the users environmental impact based on the places they shop and the types of things they purchace.
